# General Narrative Controller

Use when genre is unclear, hybrid, or not one of the three initial controllers.

Decide:

- primary type and one secondary type;
- plot density;
- clarity requirement;
- realism level;
- emotion priority;
- character continuity requirement;
- AI intervention level;
- audience prediction relationship;
- ending family.

For hybrids, one type controls narrative structure and the secondary type modifies tone or audience relation. Do not blend all genre rules equally.

Baseline:

- medium-high reality anchors;
- medium mechanism visibility;
- delayed but understandable rule/tendency;
- one primary escalation dimension plus one thematic dimension;
- ending must transform the meaning of the mechanism.
