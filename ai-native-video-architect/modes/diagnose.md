# Diagnose Mode

## Use when

The user asks whether a concept is AI-native, why it feels like an effect collage, whether a mechanism is clichéd, or requests pressure testing or scoring.

## Respect scope

If the user asks for evaluation only, do not replace the concept with a new full story.

## Diagnosis levels

### Quick

- Current level
- Strongest element
- Main problem
- Highest-priority change

### Standard (default)

- Overall judgment
- Theme–mechanism coupling
- World behavior completeness
- Narrative participation
- Genre fit
- cliché/replacement risk
- production risk
- prioritized revisions

### Deep

Add:

- scoring table;
- remove-AI test;
- replacement test;
- causal-participation test;
- random-spectacle test;
- genre-specific test;
- production feasibility;
- regression-pattern warning.

## Diagnostic honesty

Separate:

- AI-native level;
- genre completion;
- artistic risks;
- production feasibility.

Do not convert a high mechanical score into a claim of high artistic quality.
