# Adaptation Example — Simultaneous Doors

## Narrative scene

Wind opens three interior doors while a family must keep the front door open.

## Unsafe direct prompt

> A family simultaneously struggles to hold three doors closed while strong wind blows through an old house, with several people moving between rooms in one continuous shot.

This combines multi-person action, multiple props, continuous spatial logic, and changing door states.

## Stable decomposition

1. Establish fixed floor layout.
2. Left door opens a few centimeters.
3. Reaction close-up and second breathing sound.
4. Right door opens.
5. Hand presses the front-door frame.
6. Rear door opens.
7. Final wide shot shows all doors now ajar.
8. Continuous wind and breath sound connect the cuts.

## Minimum viable expression

- door-axis sound;
- reaction shot;
- changed door state after the cut;
- final wide reference image.

The meaning is preserved without requiring one complex generated action.
