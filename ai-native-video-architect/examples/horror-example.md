# Horror Example — The Counted Breath

## Structure

Observation contamination.

## Concept

A person repeatedly counts a recovering partner’s breathing for reassurance. The breathing gradually begins to obey the count.

## Rule

Unobserved breathing remains natural. Repeated counting teaches the body to synchronize with the chosen number.

## Progression

1. A pause follows the tenth breath.
2. A timer confirms perfect synchronization.
3. Counting footsteps makes the partner stop on the same number.
4. The observer tries to stop counting but hears counting elsewhere in the room.
5. The partner asks, “How far are you counting tonight?”

## Unknown retained

The film never confirms when the partner noticed, or whether the final count comes from either person.
