# AI-Native Score

Use for deep Diagnose mode or internal comparison, not as an automatic artistic ranking.

| Dimension | Max |
|---|---:|
| Theme coupling | 25 |
| World behavior completeness | 20 |
| Narrative participation | 20 |
| Evolution and closure | 15 |
| Medium non-substitutability | 15 |
| AI aesthetic self-awareness | 5 |

## Bands

- 0–29: AI tool-use
- 30–49: AI-enhanced
- 50–69: AI-driven
- 70–84: AI-native
- 85–100: highly mature AI-native

## Hard gate

AI-native requires:

- total ≥70;
- theme ≥16;
- behavior ≥12;
- participation ≥12;
- non-substitutability ≥9.

## Scoring caution

Also report separately:

- genre completion;
- artistic risks;
- production feasibility.

Do not compare the raw score of a restrained realist work with a rule-heavy horror or comedy as if it measures universal artistic quality.
