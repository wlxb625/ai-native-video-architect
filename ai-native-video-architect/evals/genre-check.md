# Genre Checks

## Realism

- Is real responsibility explicit?
- Does the abnormal reveal rather than replace the issue?
- Is the first response reality pressure rather than spectacle?
- Does the character take or refuse a concrete position?
- Is the ending avoiding automatic healing?

## Horror

- What structure is used?
- What does the creator know versus the audience?
- What remains unknown after explanation?
- Does character observation/use/avoidance affect the danger?
- Does the rule remain coherent without explaining everything?
- Is fear generated without generic monster imagery?

## Comedy

- Can the basic rule be explained quickly?
- At which appearance does the audience understand it?
- What is the joke escalation?
- What is the theme escalation?
- What is the harm threshold?
- Is the structure different from automatic loophole backlash?
- Is the ending more than recurrence?
