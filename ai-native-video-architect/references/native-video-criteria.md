# AI-Native Video Criteria

## Working definition

A video is AI-native when generative behavior directly participates in narrative, meaning, or the audience’s interpretive relationship, and removing it materially damages the work’s core expression.

## Levels

- **AI tool-use:** AI changes production efficiency only.
- **AI-enhanced:** AI adds atmosphere or visual amplification.
- **AI-driven:** major nodes rely on generative behavior.
- **AI-native:** the generative mechanism is part of the story’s operating logic.
- **Highly mature AI-native:** mechanism, form, type control, character, and production design reinforce one another.

## Six dimensions

1. Theme coupling — 25
2. World behavior completeness — 20
3. Narrative participation — 20
4. Evolution and closure — 15
5. Medium non-substitutability — 15
6. AI aesthetic self-awareness — 5

AI-native gate:

- total ≥ 70;
- theme coupling ≥ 16;
- world behavior ≥ 12;
- narrative participation ≥ 12;
- non-substitutability ≥ 9.

Do not compare raw totals across genres as artistic rankings. A restrained realist work may score lower than a hard-rule horror work while being equally mature within its type.
