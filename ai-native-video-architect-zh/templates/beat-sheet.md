# Beat Sheet Template

每个节拍必须产生事件、策略、关系、情绪、意义、视觉、声音或形式中的至少一种变化。

```yaml

climax_type:
ending_type:

beats:
  - beat_id:
    time_range:
    role:
    primary_change_type:
    entry_state:
      facts: []
      knowledge: []
      relationship:
      props: []
      reality_layer:
      formal_state:
    subject:
    goal_or_state:
    constraint:
    action_or_formal_event:
    result:
    consequence:
    knowledge_change:
    relationship_change:
    emotional_change:
    meaning_change:
    visual_change:
    sound_change:
    formal_change:
    trend_element:
    audience_question_after:
    exit_state:
    production_risk:
    stable_alternative:
    must_protect: []

middle_audit:
  what_repeats:
  what_changes:
  why_necessary:

climax_audit:
  setup_beats: []
  chooser:
  actor:
  cost:
  result:

ending_audit:
  completed:
  open:
  common_fact:
  final_action:
  final_image:
  final_sound:
```
